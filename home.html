Kat Nguyen

Hello! My name is Kat Nguyen, and I am a senior at Carlmont High School. On January 4th, 2021, I earned my Girl Scout Gold Award by establishing a tri-bin waste system at Ralston Middle School and educating students on how to sort their waste. Through this website, you can learn more about my project and how I became a Gold Award Girl Scout!
